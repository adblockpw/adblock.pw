Why block Adblock?

Adblock is a popular browser extension. The reason for the popularity is simply because it blocks out ads. Big sites such as Facebook isn't very hurt by Adblock, but small websites and blogs are. If you are running a website, and generating revenue from ads, you should force your users to disable their Adblock, while browsing your site. So in general, you will generate a stronger revenue, by using Adblock.pw.
